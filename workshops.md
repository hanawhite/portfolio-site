I run in-person writing workshops in Edinburgh.

These 2 hour workshops run for 6 weeks and consist of 6-8 people per workshop. Each person will submit and be workshopped twice throughout the course.

Each week, you will read 2-3 of your coursemate's pieces, ranging from 1,500 - 2,500 words. We will then discuss these pieces in person and you will receive written feedback from each coursemate on your piece.

- OCT-NOV 2026 - £160
- JAN-FEB 2027 - £160
- MARCH-MAY 2027 TBC
