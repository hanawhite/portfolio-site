My name is Hana and I am a homo sapien of the hominidae family and!!! a PRIMATE of the class MAMMALIA.

I am a writer based in Edinburgh working on a novel currently supported by Creative Scotland.

I also write short fiction and have been published in *3:AM*, *Extra Teeth*, and *MidCult*.

I hold an MA in Prose Fiction from UEA (with distinction), where I was a Bright Scholar.

I enjoy climbing, visual arts, and many varities of tomatoes. I hate running, the idea of running, and going for a run.

I am NOT a web designer and have ATTEMPTED to make my own website so do NOT complain about any errant bugs you may find.
